The main page of ScaleAI introduces an AI-powered system for scaling education businesses.

It features a high-impact hero section explaining how educators can automate operations and grow revenue using AI. Visitors can explore a live-style dashboard preview showing student activity, revenue growth, and AI recommendations.

The page highlights core features such as AI student support, content repurposing, lead scoring, automated onboarding, funnel analytics, and smart scheduling.

An interactive calculator allows users to estimate lost revenue and time caused by manual work. Social proof is provided through testimonials from educators across different industries.

The page concludes with a strong call to action encouraging visitors to book a strategy audit to identify automation opportunities using AI.
