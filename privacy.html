Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2025

Portfolio Project Notice

ScaleAI Systems is a portfolio demonstration project. This privacy policy outlines how data would be handled if this were a commercial service. Currently, limited data collection occurs for demonstration purposes only.

Information We Collect

For demonstration purposes, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and encrypted password when you create an account
  • Audit Data: Business descriptions and revenue information you provide for AI audits
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics on feature usage (timestamps, audit generation)

How We Use Your Information

  • • To provide the AI audit generation feature
  • • To maintain your account and audit history
  • • To demonstrate the technical capabilities of the platform
  • • To improve the AI prompt engineering and output quality

Data Storage and Security

  • • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • • Data is stored in a local SQLite database
  • • Sessions are managed with secure, httpOnly cookies
  • • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

Note: In the current deployment on Vercel, the database is ephemeral and may be reset during deployments. This is a technical limitation of the hosting setup.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: Your business descriptions are sent to OpenAI's API to generate audit recommendations. Review OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: Hosting and deployment. Review Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Tailwind CDN: CSS framework loaded from CDN.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • • Access your personal data
  • • Request deletion of your account and data
  • • Export your audit history
  • • Opt out of data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us.

Cookies

We use session cookies to maintain your authentication state. These cookies are essential for the platform to function and are deleted when you log out or close your browser.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the project evolves. Significant changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us.
